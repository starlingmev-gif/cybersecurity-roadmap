Industry Certification Strategy

Evaluating defensive security certifications based on technical rigor, hands-on relevance, and HR filtering utility.

CompTIA Security+

Goal: ATS Filter & Theoretical Baseline

Focuses primarily on security governance, risk management frameworks (NIST, ISO), and general engineering terminology. Crucial for bypassing Automated Tracking Systems (ATS) used by HR at major enterprise firms.

  • Format: Multiple-choice (theoretical)
  • Approx. Cost: ~$400 USD
  • Market Utility: Mandatory baseline for regulatory and corporate gatekeeping

BTL1 (Blue Team Level 1)

Goal: Practical Incident Triaging Capability

A rigorous, practical 24-hour examination that requires candidates to respond to simulated corporate security incident telemetry. It tests log parsing, incident investigation, and network triaging on a live cloud VM.

  • Format: 100% practical, hands-on lab assessment
  • Approx. Cost: ~$500 USD (includes lifetime course access)
  • Market Utility: Highly respected by SOC managers and incident response leads

HTB CDSA (Defensive Security Analyst)

Goal: Advanced Detection Engineering & Threat Hunting

An exhaustive, multi-day, practical exam requiring deep host analysis, forensic log parsing, and the development of high-fidelity detection rules against sophisticated threat actors.

  • Format: Deep, connected multi-day VM threat hunting lab
  • Approx. Cost: ~$450 USD
  • Market Utility: Gold standard for proving true technical depth to elite engineering teams
